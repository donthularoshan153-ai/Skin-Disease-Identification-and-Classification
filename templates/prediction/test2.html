About

Skin Disease Identification and Classification

Our AI-powered Skin Disease Identification and Classification system helps users detect and classify common skin diseases using deep learning. By simply uploading an image, the model analyzes the skin condition and provides a predicted diagnosis. Built with EfficientNetB3 and Django, this project aims to assist in early detection and awareness of skin-related issues. While it is not a substitute for medical advice, it serves as a helpful tool for preliminary analysis.
